People Before Power

Why? Because silence protects power.

Because there is a vacuum where the state has surrendered to lawlessness, where billionaires rape children and right-wing militias get away with murder. Because the 99% will fill this void and build a future for everyone.

Because by then we will have driven fascists off the face of this wonderful planet.

Our core reason

  • Power without limits turns law into a shield for the privileged.
  • Fear is political when institutions normalize violence and impunity.
  • Collective refusal works when ordinary people stop cooperating with injustice.

Reasons

What Makes This Necessary

Power and violence

  • Because we face organized violence, not normal politics.
  • Because billionaires and techno-fascists exploit every loophole, operate across borders, and act with impunity.
  • Because checks and balances have failed, and state institutions no longer restrain concentrated power.
  • Because wealth, borders, policing, and media have been turned into systems of control.

Faith and ideology

  • Because reactionary alliances with religious extremism are openly shaping policy.
  • Because proximity to fascism has become open common ground.
  • Because science denial and book bans are used to criminalize dissent and critical thinking.
  • Because faith must never be imposed as law.

Corruption and coverups

  • Because humanity deserves dignity, protection, and better than this.
  • Because the level of corruption is shameless and pervasive.
  • Because abuse protected by power is not an accident; it is a structure.
  • Because the biggest coverups reveal how deep impunity runs.

State violence and extremism

  • Because raids, murders, and fear politics must not become normal.
  • Because fascist technocrats turn human life into data and efficiency into control.
  • Because old authoritarian power returns in new language and new brands.
  • Because transnational extremist networks survive through money, media, and influence.

Borders and migration

  • Because migrants drowning at the frontier is a failure of humanity.
  • Because no border should be a death sentence.

Earth and collapse

  • Because Earth’s resources are treated as infinite while ecosystems collapse.
  • Because water scarcity becomes conflict, displacement, and inequality.
  • Because pollution and waste return as poison, loss, and extinction.
  • Because wars consume lives, land, truth, and futures.

Humanity and meaning

  • Because humanity is more than a statistic or denominator.
  • Because life is improbable and precious beyond measure.

Poverty and hunger

  • Because above all, poverty and hunger are the worst things on Earth.
  • Because they are the most basic betrayal of humanity.

Responsibility and action

Because there may be no one else watching. Because if we are the observers, responsibility begins here.

Question power. Protect life. Refuse silence. Join the movement and turn conscience into action.
